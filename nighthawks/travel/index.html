Tips & Notes
  • Fill up petrol in Newcastle before departure. Motorway services are ~20p/litre above average. Current UK average is ~158p/litre.
  • Bank holiday Friday: A1(M) southbound will be congested from midday. The 8am departure keeps you ahead of the worst of it.
  • Cruise control at 60 to 65mph on the motorway gives meaningfully better MPG than 70mph with minimal time cost.
  • For the return journey, fill up at a supermarket in Buckingham (~5 miles) rather than on the M1. Significantly cheaper.
  • The A605 Nene Valley stretch from Norman Cross to Irthlingborough is a pleasant A-road diversion, not miles added, it's the natural route.
  • On-site directions: through main gate, follow tarmac right, straight ahead to signs, turn LEFT at car park/camping sign.
  • A43 through Northamptonshire can have traffic near the Northampton ring road and around Silverstone on race weekends. Check before you go.

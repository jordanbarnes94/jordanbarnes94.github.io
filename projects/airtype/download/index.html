Quick Setup

  1. Run AirType.exe on your PC and note the IP and port shown.
  2. Install and open AirType on your Android phone.
  3. Enter your PC's IP and port, then tap Connect.
  4. Click any window on your PC and start typing from your phone.

Both devices must be on the same WiFi network.

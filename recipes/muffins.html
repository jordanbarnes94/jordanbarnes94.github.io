Ingredients

dry:
  • self raising flour, 250g
  • cocoa powder, 50g
  • chopped chocolate, 150g (+50g for topping)
wet:
  • egg, 1
  • oil, 90ml
  • milk, 250ml
  • vanilla extract, 2 tsp
  • baking powder, 1 tsp
  • white sugar, 125g
  • light brown sugar, 50g

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 200C, and line a muffin tray with 12 paper cases.
  2. Sift dry ingredients into a bowl, and stir to combine.
  3. In a jug, whisk together wet ingredients
  4. Form a well in the centre of the dry ingredients. Pour in the wet ingredients, and gently stir to combine. DO NOT OVERMIX!
  5. Spoon batter into muffin cases and sprinkle top with extra chocolate.
  6. Bake for 18 minutes, or until risen and crispy.
  7. (optional) Dust with icing sugar.

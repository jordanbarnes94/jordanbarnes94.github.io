Ingredients

  • milk, 250g
  • sugar, 35g
  • corn flour, 20g
  • cocoa powder, 14g
  • a pinch of salt
  • butter, 15g
  • vanilla, 1 teaspoon

Method

  1. Thoroughly mix together the cornflour, sugar, cocoa powder and salt. For thicker results, use more corn flour.
  2. Put the milk in a pan and add the dry ingredients.
  3. Whisking constantly, bring mixture to a simmer until it thickens.
  4. Take the pan off the heat and stir in the vanilla and butter.
  5. Pour into a bowl and let cool until it stops steaming.
  6. To prevent a skin from forming, lay cling film across the surface, then leave in the fridge for a few hours.
